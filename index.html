About ARTin

ARTin is an art exhibition platform created by Hamid and Dustin, two aspiring students dedicated to making the school environment more vibrant. This platform provides a space where students can freely express their creativity and emotions through artwork. By showcasing your artistic passion on ARTin, we hope to inspire and support your dreams beyond the boundaries of the school curriculum.
